Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is StudyVerse really free?

Yes! StudyVerse is completely free to get started. You get access to our core features, including the Pomodoro timer, basic AI assistance, and study groups.

How does the AI assistant work?

Our AI assistant is powered by Google's advanced Gemini models. It can help you understand complex topics, summarize notes, generate quizzes from your syllabus, and even give you study tips personalized to your schedule.

Can I study with my friends?

Absolutely! You can create or join public Study Rooms, invite your friends, share resources, and even challenge them to quiz battles to see who mastered the topic best.

What is the Gamification system?

We make studying addiction-free but fun. You earn XP for every minute you study, every task you complete, and every quiz you ace. Level up, unlock cool badges, and streaks to keep your momentum going!

Everything You Need to Excel

Powerful tools designed to boost your productivity and make learning engaging

AI Study Assistant

Get instant help with your studies using our Google Gemini-powered chatbot. Ask questions, get explanations, and receive personalized study tips 24/7.

Gamification System

Earn XP, level up, unlock badges, and climb the ranks from Bronze to Grandmaster. Stay motivated with streaks, achievements, and a competitive leaderboard.

Pomodoro Timer

Boost focus with scientifically-proven Pomodoro sessions. Track your productivity, earn XP for completed sessions, and maintain your study streak.

AI-Generated Quizzes

Upload your syllabus and get personalized quizzes generated by AI. Test your knowledge, track progress, and identify areas for improvement.

Battle Mode

Challenge friends to competitive quiz battles! Compete in real-time, earn XP for victories, and prove your knowledge in head-to-head matches.

Group Study Rooms

Collaborate with classmates in real-time chat rooms. Share files, discuss topics, and learn together in a supportive community environment.

Smart Task Manager

Organize your studies with priorities, categories, and syllabus integration. Set deadlines, get reminders, and track completion with ease.

Power-Up Shop

Spend earned coins on XP boosters, time multipliers, profile themes, and exclusive badges. Customize your experience and accelerate your progress.

Progress Analytics

Visualize your study patterns with detailed charts and statistics. Track focus time, completed tasks, quiz scores, and overall performance trends.
