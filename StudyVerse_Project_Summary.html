Project Overview

StudyVerse: AI-Powered Productivity Platform

StudyVerse is a comprehensive, gamified study companion designed to revolutionize how university students manage their academic journey. Built with cutting-edge AI technology and modern web frameworks, it combines productivity tools, social features, and intelligent assistance into one seamless platform.

Aim & Vision

Our Aim

To create an all-in-one academic productivity ecosystem that empowers students to achieve their full potential through intelligent task management, collaborative learning, and personalized AI guidance. We aim to transform the traditional study experience into an engaging, rewarding, and highly effective journey.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student has access to a personal AI study coach that understands their unique learning style, tracks their progress in real-time, and provides contextual support exactly when needed. StudyVerse aims to be the ultimate companion that makes studying not just productive, but genuinely enjoyable through gamification, social collaboration, and intelligent automation.

Core Philosophy

"Transform studying from a solitary struggle into a collaborative adventure, where every task completed, every minute focused, and every milestone achieved brings students closer to their academic dreams while building lasting study habits and connections."

Technical Architecture

Backend: Flask (Python) with SQLAlchemy ORM, Flask-Login for auth, Flask-SocketIO for real-time features

Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (custom design system), Vanilla JavaScript with modern ES6+ features

Database: SQLite (development) / PostgreSQL (production) with 20+ models and relationships

AI Integration: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash for chat, PDF analysis, quiz generation, and battle judging

Real-Time: WebSocket (SocketIO) for group chat, battle synchronization, and live updates

Deployment: Render cloud platform with WhiteNoise for static files, ProxyFix for HTTPS
